## Information Collection and Use

### What We DON'T Collect

NoteOnGo AI does **NOT** collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

- ‚ùå Voice recordings
- ‚ùå Transcribed text
- ‚ùå Notes, tasks, events, or reminders
- ‚ùå Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- ‚ùå Location data
- ‚ùå Usage analytics
- ‚ùå Device identifiers
- ‚ùå Any other personal data

### How Your Data is Processed

**All processing happens on your device:**

1. **Voice Recording**: When you record a voice note, the audio is saved locally on your device only.

2. **Transcription**: Speech-to-text conversion uses Apple's on-device Speech Recognition API. Your audio never leaves your device.

3. **AI Analysis**: Entity extraction and categorization is performed by an on-device language model (LLM) that runs entirely on your iPhone. No data is sent to external servers.

4. **Storage**: All data (recordings, transcripts, tasks, events, reminders, shopping lists, and notes) is stored in a local SQLite database on your device.
